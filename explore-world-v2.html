📈 Core Global Health Indicators

Under-5 Mortality

Deaths before age 5 per 1,000 live births.

Inequities reflect access to care, nutrition, and sanitation.

Neonatal Mortality

Deaths in the first 28 days of life.

Reflects maternal care and health system quality.

Life Expectancy

Average expected lifespan under current conditions.

Reflects overall social and health conditions.

Vaccination Coverage

Percentage of population receiving essential vaccines.

Key indicator of prevention systems.

Adult Obesity

BMI ≥ 30 prevalence among adults.

Reflects lifestyle and environmental factors.

Air Pollution

Exposure to PM2.5 and environmental risks.

Strong SDH and environmental health indicator.
