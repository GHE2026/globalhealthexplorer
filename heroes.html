Why Global Health Heroes Matter

Global health heroes show us that ideas, compassion, and action can change lives. Their work inspires us to think beyond ourselves and consider the health of communities, countries, and the world.

🌍 Hear a Global Health Voice

Mohammad Asadi-Lari’s (1996–2020) TEDx talk, "Empowering Youth to Think Global but Act Local", was delivered at TEDxYouth@WestVancouver in British Columbia in 2019. In this presentation, he shares his experience as an immigrant adapting to life in Canada and discusses how young people can become active leaders in their communities by engaging locally while thinking globally aligning with the GHE guiding principle: Think globally, act locally. Do it now.
