Global Health Heroes

People, institutions, and systems that shaped global health.

Global health heroes include scientists, leaders, communities, and institutions that generate knowledge, produce evidence, and shape global health systems.

Two Layers of GH Heroes

(1) People who transformed health science and practice
(2) Institutions that produce global health evidence and data
