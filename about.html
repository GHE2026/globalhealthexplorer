About the Founder

Global Health Explorers was founded by Zoha Asadi-Lari to inspire children and adolescents to learn about global health and become future leaders committed to improving health, wellbeing, equity, and sustainability. The platform is built on the belief that informed young people can contribute to healthier communities, stronger societies, and a better future for all.

✨ Honouring a Legacy of Dedication

This platform is deeply rooted in a profound family commitment to public health, leadership, and global improvement. It seeks to highlight, carry forward, and revive the wonderful paths, names, and legacies of Dr Mohammad Hossein, and Zeynab Asadi-Lari, Zoha's beloved siblings, who were brutally killed among 176 innocent passengers and crew of Flight PS752, which was intentionally downed on Jan 8th 2020, ensuring their passion for a better world continues to inspire the next generation of global health leaders.
