Explore the World

Understand global health patterns across countries, cultures, and systems.

The world becomes understandable through evidence. Global health data helps us recognize differences in health, understand their causes, and identify inequities across populations.

Evidence Hub Principle

GHE teaches learners to understand evidence, recognize inequities, and ask thoughtful questions before proposing solutions.

How learners use evidence

Trusted global institutions such as WHO, UNICEF, UNFPA, PAHO, CDC, IHME, and national agencies provide interpreted health evidence that helps us understand global patterns.
