Understanding Indigenous perspectives is essential to understanding health in Canada. This section explores holistic wellness frameworks rooted in community, traditional knowledge, and respect.

🌲 Special Focus: First Peoples of British Columbia

We honor and highlight the unique contributions of the First Peoples of British Columbia to health, culture, and community wellbeing. Their timeless frameworks offer vital paths toward a more sustainable and balanced future for healthcare.

Core Learning Topics

  • Indigenous Concepts of Wellness: Exploring holistic health views encompassing mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual balance.
  • Community and Connection: Understanding how intergenerational relationships and kinship build collective health resilience.
  • Traditional Knowledge: Appreciating ancestral land management, plant remedies, and ecological wisdom.
  • Health Equity: Analyzing structural challenges and creating paths to overcome healthcare disparities affecting Indigenous communities.
  • Reconciliation and Respect: Partnering together in meaningful ways to build an inclusive, equitable future for all public wellness programs.
