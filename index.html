Welcome to Global Health Explorers

Learning Global Health Today, Leading Tomorrow

Our Vision

A world where every young person has the opportunity to learn about global health, appreciate the interconnectedness of people and communities, and become an active contributor to improving health and wellbeing locally and globally.

Our Mission

To empower children and adolescents with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration needed to understand global health challenges, make informed decisions, and contribute to a healthier, more equitable, and sustainable world.

About the Founders

Global Health Explorers was co-founded by Zoha Asadi-Lari and Matin Moradkhan to inspire children and adolescents to learn about global health and become future leaders committed to improving health, wellbeing, equity, and sustainability. The platform is built on the belief that informed young people can contribute to healthier communities, stronger societies, and a better future for all.

✨ Honouring a Legacy of Dedication

This platform is deeply rooted in a profound family commitment to public health, leadership, and global improvement. It seeks to highlight, carry forward, and revive the wonderful paths, names, and legacies of Dr Mohammad Hossein, and Zeynab Asadi-Lari, Zoha's beloved siblings, who were brutally killed among 176 innocent passengers and crew of Flight PS752, which was intentionally downed on Jan 8th 2020, ensuring their passion for a better world continues to inspire the next generation of global health leaders.
