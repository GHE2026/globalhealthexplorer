Building Canada’s next generation of global health leaders.

An educational platform empowering children and adolescents to understand global health, explore real-world challenges, and become active contributors to healthier and more equitable communities.

Our Vision

A world where every young person has the opportunity to learn about global health, appreciate the interconnectedness of people and communities, and become an active contributor to improving health and wellbeing locally and globally.

Our Mission

To empower children and adolescents with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration needed to understand global health challenges, make informed decisions, and contribute to a healthier, more equitable, and sustainable world.
