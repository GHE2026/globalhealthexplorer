Welcome to Global Health Explorers

An educational platform designed to help children and adolescents understand the factors that influence health and wellbeing around the world.

Learning Global Health Today, Leading Tomorrow

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To empower children and adolescents with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration needed to understand global health challenges, make informed decisions, and contribute to a healthier, more equitable, and sustainable world.

Our Vision: A world where every young person has the opportunity to learn about global health, appreciate the interconnectedness of people and communities, and become an active contributor to improving health and wellbeing locally and globally.
