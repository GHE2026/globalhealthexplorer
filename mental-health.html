🧠 Mental Health

Understanding emotions, wellbeing, resilience, and the social conditions that shape mental health across the world.

🌍 Global Health Lens

Why is Mental Health a Global Health Topic?

Mental health is a global health issue because social conditions, conflict, poverty, education, discrimination, and access to quality care shape mental wellbeing across populations and throughout the life course.

📊 Global Health Snapshot

Global evidence shows that mental disorders are among the leading causes of illness and disability worldwide. The number of people living with mental disorders has increased from approximately 600 million in 1990 to about 1.17 billion today.

Why It Matters

Mental health affects how we think, learn, connect with others, and contribute to society. Improving mental health is essential for building inclusive, healthy, and resilient communities worldwide.
