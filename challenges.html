Global Health Big Data Challenge

A flagship learning initiative where young people analyze real-world health data, identify patterns, and design innovative, data-driven solutions for global health challenges.

Program Overview

1. Explore Data

Understand global health datasets, indicators, and population-level patterns.

2. Analyze Problems

Identify key health challenges using evidence-based analytical thinking.

3. Build Solutions

Develop innovative, scalable solutions using data-driven insights.

4. Communicate Impact

Present findings in clear, compelling, and policy-relevant formats.

Who Can Participate?

The GHBDC is open to students, educators, and young innovators interested in global health, data science, and social impact innovation.

  • Secondary school students
  • University undergraduates
  • Teachers and mentors
  • Youth innovation groups
