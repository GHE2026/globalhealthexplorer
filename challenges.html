🌍 Global Health Big Data Challenge

Turning Data into Action. Youth into Changemakers.

The Global Health Big Data Challenge invites children and adolescents across Canada to explore real-world health problems using data, creativity, and systems thinking.

Participants work individually or in teams to uncover patterns, generate evidence-informed insights, and design innovative solutions to some of the most pressing health challenges facing our world.

⚡ The Global Action & Advocacy Arena

🏆 Case Hackathons & Innovation Labs

Collaborative hackathon models testing students to engineer scalable local policy solutions addressing dynamic disease crises or clinical equity gaps.

🗣️ Global Health Forums & Debates

Structured forensic debate spaces evaluating public health ethical conundrums, vaccine allocations, and international clinical resource financing protocols.

💡 Public Policy Presentations & Spotlights
